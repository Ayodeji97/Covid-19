Stay Safe

Personal Hygiene

Wash your hands often with soap and water or use hand sanitizer for at least 20 second after you have been to a public place,or after blowing your nose, coughing, or sneezing. Clean and disinfect frequently touched surfaces daily.

Over Crowd

Prevent the gathering of a lot of people. Cover your mouth and nose with a cloth or face cover when around others. Remember that some people without symptoms may be able to spread the virus, hence, keep a enough distance between yourslf and others.

Rules & Regulations

Adhere strictly to the rules and regulations laid down by government and health organisation. Stay at home as much as possible. Avoid unneccesary movement and contact. Cooperate with Our government.
